User Guide

Drawing a Buffer

  1. Click the "Buffer" menu on the bottom toolbar in the left hand corner of OLIVER. The default is to draw a buffer in meters. To change the unit of measurement, click on one of the listed units. Enter a number (decimal is acceptable) into the "Buffer radius" box. Then, click on the map to draw the buffer.



    2. The buffer will appear as a partially transparent orange circle.



    To clear the buffer circle from the map, click on the "Clear measurement" button to the left of the buffer tool.
